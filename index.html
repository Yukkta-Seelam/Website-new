About Me

I'm pursuing my Master's in Robotics and Autonomous Systems at Boston University, focusing on Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, and Autonomous Systems. My most recent experience was with Red Hat, where I developed efficient real-world reinforcement learning systems using hybrid local-cloud inference.

Currently based in Boston, MA, I'm actively seeking full-time opportunities in Robotics, AI, Machine Learning, and Automation. I value strategic thinking both professionally and in my hobbies. I'm always up for a good challenge, whether it's debugging reinforcement learning policies or trading sheep for wood.
