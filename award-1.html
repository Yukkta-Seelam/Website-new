3rd Place, DJI Robomaster AI Challenge

IEEE ICRA DJI

May 2022

In a team of 5, developed intelligent solutions for various research problems of robotics by using a fusion of multi Lidar system and visual odometry under high-speed motion, accurate robot detection & shooting. Proposal landed in the top 5 proposals of the competition, while the team fetched 3rd position in the main competition, as the only Indian team in the competition.
