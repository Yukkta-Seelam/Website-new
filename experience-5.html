Robotics Research Intern

@ Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur

Aug 2023 - Mar 2024

Overview

Developed perception-based obstacle avoidance algorithms for an autonomous rover by integrating real-time sensor data with ROS-based A* and TEB planners, enabling safe, reliable navigation through dynamic and unstructured environments.

Implemented a YOLOv5-based armor plate classification system, achieving 85% mAP, that formed the perception backbone for the rover's targeting pipeline.

Integrated the classification system with a PID-controlled gimbal for accurate target tracking, allowing the rover to autonomously track and engage targets while in motion.

ROS A* Planner TEB Planner YOLOv5 Classification PID Control Path Planning
